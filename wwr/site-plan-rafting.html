Overview

Purpose

The purpose of establishing an online presence for our rafting business is to effectively market our services and streamline the booking process. Through our digital platform, we aim to attract potential customers, showcase our unique offerings, and provide easy access for booking and reservations, thus enhancing customer engagement and driving business growth.

Audience

Who are the target customers?
 Our target customers are adventure enthusiasts aged 18 to 40, with varying financial situations, who seek thrilling outdoor experiences like water rafting.
What do they want?
They want thrilling and memorable experiences in nature, seeking excitement and challenge through rafting adventures.
What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?
They expect comprehensive information on trip details, safety measures, and booking options, along with visuals and testimonials.
What are their needs that aren't being met?
They struggle to find reliable rafting companies offering thrilling yet safe experiences with personalized service and easy booking processes.
How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)?
They will primarily access the site via mobile devices for on-the-go browsing but may also use desktop or laptop computers for more in-depth planning.
